Vertical Slice Project

My Tower Defense game was a project I did during the first period of my second year. This is a project I had to do alone. During this project I really started to apply code conventions and best practices. Like single use responsibility. This project taught me a lot about how to use them effectively.

Within the project I worked on a lot of different parts, including the story, a big part of the ui, the main menu and dialogue. The story is about someone who gets layed off from their job. He goes to the beach to discover a note in which he learns about a way to get any wish he wants if he goes to a Hawaiian volcano. He goes there and meets the goddess of the sea who in return for stealing Pele's treasure the Lava Stone he'll get his wish.

Some of the scripts I've made are about the dialogue system. Showing different images after reaching a certain point in the story. I've also made the level system, where after surviving for a while you reach the next level. I also scripted the whole main menu including a settings menu with a slider for sound which worked across the entire game. I am also the one who put the levels together with the art we got from the artists.

Mythe was one of my favorite projects I've worked on. I learned a lot from it and the game we made is something I am proud of. Over here you can check out the Github link.
