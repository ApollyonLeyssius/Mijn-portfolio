About Me

My name is Julian van der Leek, I am 19 years old and enjoy developing games just as much as I enjoy playing them. I was born in Haarlem and currently live in Cruquius. I've lived in Ireland for a year, so my English is at a native level. I am also studying Japanese and am already at N4 level.

A big reason why I chose this study is my passion for amazing games. I've also got a passion for lore and writing. In the games I've developed thus far, I've made scripts as well as the story.

A big inspiration for me is Clair Obscur: Expedition 33. The way they tell an amazing story with amazing gameplay, all made by a passionate team of 30 people, is an amazing feat and it is my dream to be able to work on a project like it.
